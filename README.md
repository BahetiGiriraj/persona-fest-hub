# Persona Fest 2026 🎉  
## MIT ADT University

Persona Fest is the flagship techno-cultural festival of MIT ADT University — a grand celebration of innovation, creativity, culture, and academic excellence.

This project is a modern, responsive festival website designed to showcase events, highlights, speakers, competitions, registrations, and the overall experience of Persona Fest.

---

# 🌟 About Persona Fest

Persona Fest is one of India’s premier techno-cultural festivals where creativity meets technology.  
Over three exciting days, the festival brings together students, institutions, artists, innovators, and industry leaders under one platform.

The fest combines:
- Technology
- Culture
- Innovation
- Design
- Management
- Media & Entertainment

into a large-scale immersive university experience.
